About

Established in 2010, TravelXpert is a complete-service travel agency that specializes in both domestic and international travel planning. We offer an end-to-end travel solution including but not limited to tickets booking, exclusive hotel stay and economical car rentals. Our goal is to make both domestic and international travel comfortable and accessible for everyone.

We are a reputed travel company backed by a vast base of happy and satisfied customers. We are industry leaders in planning travel for business, special occasions, relaxation, study, family visits, etc.

Our wide range of services include -

  • Ticket Booking both domestic and international
  • Visa confirmation test
  • Visa filing
  • Documentation
  • Free counseling
  • Student Assessment
  • Interview Preparation
  • Offer Letter
